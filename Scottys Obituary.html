Obituary for Scotty Obata

Scotty Obata age 15 formerly of Nesconset has passed away.
Scotty was born in Seattle, Washington, and immediately
stole our hearts. As a young pup, Scotty enjoyed running
at speeds of 27 MPH while we chased after him using his
cunning mind to steal our socks. He also enjoyed napping
and hunting baby rabbits. He was a smart boy and many
times used his intelligence to trick us into giving him
more treats. We will miss him dearly. I pray wherever he
went, I will see him again soon.
